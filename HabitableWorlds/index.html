About

The Planetary Archive is maintained by Exoplanet Corporation, the Terranian organisation dedicated to exploring and understanding worlds beyond the Hexan System. It catalogues all known exoplanets discovered through advanced telescopes and interstellar probes, providing detailed information on their characteristics, habitability, and potential for Terranian colonisation.


Planetary exploration is guided by the Terranian Interplanetary Research Authority (TIRA), which focuses on the detection of extraterrestrial life, the study of biosignatures, and the ethical assessment of new worlds. Together, TIRA and Exoplanet Corporation work to identify Terrania-class planets, study their environments, and prepare for humanity's future among the stars.


This archive is your window into the next frontier - a growing collection of candidate worlds that may one day become Terrania 2.0.
